## Weekly Post Renamed
First, you'll notice this series has a new name — originally should be Weekly Post. In high school, to prepare for entrance exam, so from some point, Chinese teacher started requiring us to write weekly posts. But not every week has something interesting to record. When material is scarce, everyone forced to experience life — here "experience life" isn't that internet-cafe-beauty kind like find a cafe to take photos and relax, but vividly record every moment of life. Even things like squeezing bus, happening every day, written as article to turn in. Though forced, but did improve observation skill. Originally thought, already wrote like this for months in high school, now should be no problem? But after writing for a while, discovered twice a week is already limit. Especially when busy like now, one post a month is already good. Because of above reasons, decided to change the name. As for why changed to "地球online" (Earth Online) — this game-like name, greatly influenced by [Alex Hsu's game mindset](https://alexhsu.com/game-mindset). This whole series is interesting — method of leveling up in games perfectly投射 to improving yourself. Those interested can follow link to finish reading.
