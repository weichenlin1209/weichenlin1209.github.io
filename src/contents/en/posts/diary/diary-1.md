## Southern Welcome Event

The department holds a welcome event for new students before enrollment—North, Central, and South each have one. This year's participation was low, ~~probably because everyone thought the phone calls were scams.~~ Because of the lack of hands, the person in charge kept persuading me over dinner the night before. Plus I really wanted to surprise the two friends I hadn't seen in a long time. After hesitating for a while, I finally paid for the ticket one minute before the booking deadline—a day trip to Tainan. To avoid being spotted, I hid at Shin Kong Mitsukoshi until the time came. At the meetup, the plan was a huge success: they: **_ "What are you doing here!!!"_** The train fare was worth it. But in the end, we only took a photo at the station as a memento.

## Eating Eating Eating

Before school started, I didn't do much—just ate good food every day.

### [**Yongxin Desserts**](https://www.google.com/maps/place/%E6%B0%B8%E5%BF%83%E7%94%9C%E5%93%81/@24.1302728,120.6839958,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m6!3m5!1s0x34693de82039eabb:0xd208938ef7905255!8m2!3d24.1302728!4d120.6839958!16s%2Fg%2F11x92d83nr?entry=ttu&g_ep=EgoyMDI1MDkxNi4wIKXMDSoASAFQAw%3D%3D)
